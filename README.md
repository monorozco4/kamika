# kamaki



This application is to share with all the nerds out there who loves videogames and random facts
about them, such as when it was released or the prices it has won. It also marks when it was
released in different consoles and the year, along with their genre in case you are looking
for new games you want to play! Search for new games to play and have fun with!

## Features

- **Varied Catalog**: Videogames from all consoles. You can choose what you like!
- **Quick Search**: You can easily search videogames and their genre, along with the year it was released
- **Personalized Recommendations**: kamika helps you find new videogames based on your search history.
- **Intuitive Interface**: Navigate through the app effortlessly with its clean and accessible design.
